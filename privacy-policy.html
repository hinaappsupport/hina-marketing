Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Your Privacy is Our Priority: Hina is committed to protecting your child's health information. We believe privacy is a fundamental right, and we take it seriously.

1. Introduction

Hina ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Hina mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Hina, you may provide:

  • Child Information: Name, age, date of birth
  • Health Data: Daily nutrition logs, bowel movement records, urination habits, symptoms, and medical observations
  • Doctor Information: Doctor names, clinic details, appointment dates
  • Medical Notes: Your observations and notes about your child's health

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We may collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, frequency of use, app performance metrics
  • Language Preference: Your selected language (Arabic or English)

2.3 Information NOT Collected

Hina does NOT collect:

  • Location data or GPS information
  • Camera or photo library access
  • Contact information or phone numbers
  • Social media accounts or identifiers
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for:

  • App Functionality: To provide the core features of Hina (tracking, charts, tips)
  • Data Storage: To save your child's health records locally on your device
  • Personalization: To customize the app experience (language, layout)
  • App Improvement: To understand how users interact with the app and improve features
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not send your child's health information to external servers or cloud services. Your data remains entirely under your control.

4.2 Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted local storage on your device
  • Secure coding practices to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular security updates
  • No transmission of sensitive data over the internet

4.3 Your Responsibility

Since data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for:

  • Keeping your device secure with a strong password or biometric lock
  • Backing up your data regularly
  • Protecting your device from unauthorized access

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data with third parties. Your child's health information is never sold, rented, or shared with:

  • Marketing companies
  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms
  • Insurance companies
  • Healthcare providers (unless you explicitly choose to share)

6. Third-Party Services

Hina may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns (anonymized data only)
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues
  • App Distribution: Google Play Store (subject to their privacy policies)

These services may have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Children's Privacy

Hina is designed for parents/guardians to track their children's health. We:

  • Do not collect information directly from children
  • Do not allow children to create accounts or access the app independently
  • Require parental/guardian consent for all data collection
  • Comply with children's privacy regulations (COPPA, GDPR)

8. Your Rights and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Modify: Edit or update your child's information
  • Delete: Remove data from the app at any time
  • Export: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable optional features or analytics

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device as long as you keep it. When you delete data from the app, it is permanently removed. If you uninstall the app, all data stored locally is deleted (unless you have backed it up).

10. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Requesting your consent if required by law

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@hina-app.com
  • Mailing Address: Hina Support Team, [Your Address]
  • In-App Support: Use the contact form in the app settings

13. Legal Compliance

Hina complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) - USA
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - USA
  • Local privacy laws in your jurisdiction

14. Disclaimer

Important: Hina is a health tracking application and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider regarding your child's health concerns. Never delay seeking medical advice because of information provided in this app.

Your Trust Matters: We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security. Your child's health information is sacred, and we treat it with the utmost care and respect.
